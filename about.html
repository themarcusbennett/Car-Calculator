Origin Behind the App

The idea for this application first came to mind, when a friend of mine was going through a car buying dilemma. There were a number of factors that could influence their decision of buying a new car.

  1. Gas Consumption
  2. Car Payments
  3. Insurance

The ultimate question was, when would the newly bought car start to save them more money?

For example...
If you spend $20 a week on gas with your current car, but a different car could save you $10 on gas a week. But now you have a car note of $60 a month which will be payed off in 6 months, after 6 months you'll start to save money with the newly bought car.
